Invoicing to Settement Flow

Supply Goods

Stockist receives confirmed order post Field and Counter approval Order Copy can be seen by stockist on:

Sample Email Order Copy
On MedVol Portal

Stockist supplies the stock from Rack-stock

Has full Stock

Supply in Full to Counter

Has Partial Supply

Supply partially and procure remaining and supply as per guidance from Field Team

Has No stock

Procure from Depot at PTS and supply

Upload Invoice

Stockist uploads the invoice on MedVol Portal against respective Order.
Note – Bulk upload single invoice against multiple order is possible.

Validation of invoice by MedVol

Team MedVol validates the invoice and status changed to ‘Fully Invoiced’ or ‘Partially Invoiced’

If invoice is rejected email intimation is send to stockist. Further action only on clarification received by stockist.

Validation is completed in 24-48hours barring exceptions

Video link on how to check rejection and reason for rejection.

Generate Purchase order to depot

MedVol sends Purchase Order (PO) to Company Depot as per agreed schedule and frequency.
Automatically all Status ‘Invoiced’ is send on PO and no follow-up for the same is required.

Depot bill the products

Depot to bill the product and dispatch to stockist as per the SOP / Protocol

Reverse update of billing information on MedVol portal

"Company share billing information with MedVol on same day or with a delay of 1-week
Updated on MedVol portal within 48-hours of receipt

With MedVol reference PO number, Field team can check status of stockist billing at Depot

MedVol update the billing information for visibility of Field team and Stockist

Exceptional / Credit note settlement

Goods may not get billed from Depot because of?

1

Product not available

2

Product been phased out

3

Depot not to bill to stockist as per SOP

MedVol to ensure these settlement: As per SOP of the Company
via Credit Note (CN) settlement in next-month
Status of same can be checked by stockist

MedVol update the billing information for visibility of Field team and Stockist

Ready reckoner for Field Team to understand primary billing

  • Expected PO before the issuance of PO
  • PO issued
  • Check Primary Billed via MedVol
  • Shortage yet to be settled
